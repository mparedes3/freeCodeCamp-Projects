John Cruz

A drum major, friend, and a brother


Here we see John Cruz after doing his drum major routine.

A few awesome facts about him:

  • Freshman year: began to practice for drum major and joined marching band as a bass clarinet
  • Freshman year: tried out for drum major, failed but persisted
  • Sophomore year: kept practicing for dru major and bass clarinet
  • Junior year: bass clarinet trio first made, he led it
  • Junior year: during the summer, he trained the bass clarinets how to march and play well, bonded with them
  • Junior year: learned a lot as a leader
  • Junior year: tried out for drum major again, and made it
  • Senior year: began to conduct for field and street, many people looked up to him
  • Senior year: set the tone for a good band competition, encouraged many people to persevere
  • Senior year: graduated, and left a big mark on the marching band
